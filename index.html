Medellín Fashion Week

This 45-minute documentary explores the idea of beauty in Pablo Escobar's former stomping grounds of Medellín, Colombia. I called dozens of plastic surgeons, finally securing one who allowed us to film a live gluteoplasty. I also played the role of interpreter, translating from Spanish to English and vice versa during interviews. I booked all guests and interviewees and took care of production planning.

Playgiarize

For my first project in GA's Web Development Immersive course, I built a tool for slackers everywhere. Playgiarize takes each word inputted and switches it to a synonym of that word.

I won't vouch for the viability of its plagiarized response. Let's just say that this app could benefit quite a bit from a linguistics consultant. Working on that.

It's written in Ruby on the backend with a Postgres database. I used the Wordnik gem as a thesaurus. On the front end, I used the Bootstrap grid system.
