An exploration of the

non-physical interactions

that make up our love lives.

In the absence of physical closeness, how do you build a narrative and sustain the love that you have with someone else through just words or other snippets of communication?

Loving Long-Distance is a project that explores these communications through a video series and an app.

The videos tell the stories of two individuals and their attempts to bridge physical distances with written accounts.

The app helps you, the viewer, revisit the stories and memories you shared with a loved one via emails, chat messages, photos and Facebook likes.
